# Code of Conduct

AI Shop OS is intended to be a practical, respectful open-source project for ecommerce operations automation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Discuss ideas with evidence.
- Keep product criticism focused on user value, safety, workflow quality, and maintainability.
- Respect merchants, operators, maintainers, and contributors with different levels of technical experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private data, credentials, customer information, or platform secrets.
- Encouraging unauthorized scraping, account abuse, or unsafe automation.
- Repeatedly pushing features that violate the product safety boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its users.
